# System Requirements

## Functional Requirements

FR1:
The system shall monitor a predefined area.

FR2:
The drones shall maintain communication with neighbouring drones.

FR3:
The system shall continue operating if one drone fails.

FR4:
The system shall detect and report potential intrusions.

## Non-Functional Requirements

NFR1:
System response time < 5 seconds.

NFR2:
Coverage of at least 95% of the protected area.

NFR3:
Scalable to 50+ drones.

NFR4:
Able to operate autonomously for extended periods.
